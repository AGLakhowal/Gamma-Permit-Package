TITLE: NIST AI RMF PROFILE FOR GAMMA RUNTIME ENFORCEMENT (v1.1)

1. GOVERN FUNCTION (GOVERN 1.2)
The Gamma Runtime Governance Engine maps organizational risk appetite and 
institutional policies to deterministic, per-action execution predicates. 
It ensures that policy is technically enforced at the point of actuation.

2. MEASURE FUNCTION (MEASURE 2.1)
The ERTuple audit trail provides a machine-readable measurement of:
- System Integrity (ICS)
- Policy Coherence Index (I_PHI)
- Uncertainty Confidence Intervals (CI_WIDTH)
- Decision Entropy (H_X)

3. MANAGE FUNCTION (MANAGE 2.4)
Gamma provides the mandatory "Safe-Halt" and "Fail-Closed" mechanism 
(Γ > 0) required for managing high-risk AI deployments where human 
oversight alone is insufficient to prevent rapid-scale harms.

4. ALIGNMENT
A G-0 Certified system is natively compliant with the NIST AI Risk 
Management Framework (AI RMF 1.0) Core.
